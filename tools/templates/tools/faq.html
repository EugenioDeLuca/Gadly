FAQ

Find answers to common questions.

General

What is Gadly?

Gadly is a collection of free online utilities for text editing, social media, finance, web/SEO, file management, and more. All tools run in your browser—no installation required.

Is my data safe?

Most tools process data locally in your browser or send it only for the specific operation (e.g., translation, meta tag check). We do not store or log your personal data. For sensitive content, always check the privacy policy.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes! The site is responsive and optimized for smartphones and tablets. You can use all tools from your mobile device.

Text Tools

Word Counter

Paste your text and click "Count Words" or "Count Characters" to get instant counts. Useful for essays, blog posts, and character-limited platforms.

Remove Spaces

Choose a mode: remove all spaces or collapse multiple spaces into one. Great for cleaning pasted text.

Username / Bio / Hashtag / Caption Generator

Select options (style, platform, niche) and click Generate. Use the Copy button to copy the result to your clipboard.

Social Media & Video

Video Downloader

Paste a video URL from a supported site, click Download, and wait for the file. Large videos may take a few moments.

Finance Calculators

How to use the calculators?

Select the tab (Percentage, VAT, Currency, Loan, etc.), enter the values in the fields, and click the calculate button. Results appear below.

Currency conversion

Enter the amount and the exchange rate (e.g., 1 EUR = 1.08 USD). The result shows the converted amount.

Web / SEO

Meta Tag Checker

Enter a URL to see the meta tags (title, description, Open Graph, Twitter cards) of that page.

Sitemap Extractor

Paste the URL of a sitemap.xml file to extract all listed URLs. Useful for SEO audits.

Robots.txt Generator

Configure User-agent, Disallow/Allow paths, and Sitemap URL to generate a robots.txt file. Copy the result and paste it into your site's root.
